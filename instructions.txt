			HOW TO PLAY

PREVENT THE ZOMBIE HORDE FROM GETTING TO THE OTHER
SIDE OF THE BOARD. TO WIN YOU HAVE TO DEFEND USING
PLANTS THAT ATTACK ZOMBIES.

> SELECT THE LEVEL YOU WANT TO PLAY.
> PLACE YOUR PLANTS STRATEGICALLY ACCORDING TO PLANT
  TYPE.
> EACH PLANT HAS DIFFERENT CHARACTERISTICS AND ATTACK
  DAMAGE.
> YOU HAVE LIMITED MONEY TO SPEND ON PLANTS, USE IT WISELY.
> ONCE THE PLANTS ARE PLACED, THE GAME WILL START.
